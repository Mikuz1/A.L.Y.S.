About

A.L.Y.S.

A.L.Y.S. is an electronic music project from Ukraine, created by Andriy Lushchak, also known as Andy Suncraft, and Yorick Serkez, also known as DJ Base-T. Built around a shared passion for melodic electronic music, the project combines emotional songwriting, atmospheric sound design, and the driving energy of modern dance music.

The sound of A.L.Y.S. moves between EDM, progressive house, electro house, progressive trance anduplifting trance with a strong focus on melody, emotion, and memorable vocal hooks. Rather than following one fixed formula, A.L.Y.S. aims to create tracks that feel both powerful on the dancefloor and meaningful to the listener. Their music often blends uplifting chord progressions, warm synth layers, energetic grooves, and carefully crafted arrangements designed for both club and radio formats.

Service

Mixing

We work on each track in detail - balancing levels, working with panning, EQ and effects to ensure that all elements of the track sound clean, clear and harmonious. This is a key stage to ensure that the track is ready for mastering.

Mastering

We polish your track by adding clarity, depth, and the right loudness so it sounds professional on any speakers — from headphones to big club sound systems.

Sound Design

We create unique sound elements — synthesized leads, effects, bass lines, and atmospheric textures that make your music recognizable and expressive.

Music Production

We help structure a track — from an idea to a finished musical canvas with melodies, rhythms and harmonies according to the genre and your creative concept.

Remix & DJ Edit

We rework tracks for a DJ set or remix them for a club sound, preserving the energy and drive of dance music.

Vocal Processing

We clean and tune your vocal recordings for a smooth, polished sound. We add harmonies, effects, and spatial depth to fit the track's energy. Your vocals become clear, expressive, and ready for a professional release.
